About Me

A father, husband, attorney, and programmer, Florian W.M. Kreuk, Esq. has been working in and around technology for the last 10 years. With experiences ranging from a billion dollar software company and an NYC fin-tech start-up, Florian is able to support a wide range of clients and practice areas.

Leveraging his legal background and programming competence, Florian finds a niche as a solutions architect for personal injury law firms. The legal industry is experiencing a technological revolution, where the firms that invest in technology are achieving results never before imaginable.
